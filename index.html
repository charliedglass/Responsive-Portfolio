About Me

Hello! My name is Charlie Glass. I was born and raised in New York City, and I moved out to Los Angeles 4 years ago when I started studying at UCLA. I graduated from UCLA in the summer of 2018 with a B.S. in Mathematics/Economics and a Specialization in Computing. Following graduation, I started my current job as an Associate Business Intelligence Analyst at Blackwood Seven, a company that uses advanced analytics and modeling to advise and execute clients’ media plans. Previously, I worked in Data Analytics for Music Audience Exchange and the Los Angeles Kings. I am currently enrolled in UCLA Extension’s Full-Stack Web Development Bootcamp, where I hope to gain the skills necessary to build web tools for my data projects.

I am deeply passionate about sports. I try to play basketball everyday, as well as coach middle schoolers in basketball when I am free. Growing up, I became a die-hard Knicks and Yankees fan, so I have experienced both sides of the fan spectrum! I hope to one day combine my passion for sports and data and obtain a job in sports analytics either in baseball or basketball!
